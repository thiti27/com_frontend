About Us

Home > About Us





Daicel Corporation is major manufacturer of safety systems (Inflator and Pretensioner Gas Generator (PGG))and related components like an initiator and coolant in the world.

We DSST/DSTT, has been established as second overseas business base for Inflator in 2002 (DSST) and for Initiator in 2005 (DSTT).
We have market territory whole Asian except Japan, China Taiwan and Korea in Daicel group.

We have two companies, Daicel Safety Systems (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (DSST) and Daicel Safety Technologies (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (DSTT).
We are in two companies by each purpose, DSST for manufacturing of inflator and related components and DSTT for Initiator and Pretensioner Gas Generator (PGG).

With our slogan “We Save Lives”, our team members are endeavoring to supply the most competitive product with the highest safety / quality in the market.
It is our great honor and pleasure to establish our business in Thailand, and to experience the contributions of its highly skilled
and motivated people as we achieve continued growth and development through our environment-friendly product.


President Of DSST & DSTT

President’s Message

Our Companies, Daicel Safety Systems (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (DSST) and Daicel Safety Technologies (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (DSTT) have been established with the aim of becoming a leading manufacturer and sales company of inflators for airbag systems, Pretensioner Gas Generators (PGG) for seat belts and related components (Initiators, Coolants and Gas Generant) for automotive safety systems.

With our slogan “We Save Lives” our team members are endeavoring to supply the most competitive products with the highest quality in the market. It is our great honor and pleasure to establish our business in Thailand, and to experience the contributions of its highly skilled and motivated people as we achieve continuous growth and development through our environmentally friendly products.
